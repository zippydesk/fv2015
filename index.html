What you should expect

An inside look into the latest ideas and practices in front-end technologies that shape the Internet of today and tomorrow. The event begins with 30-minute presentations that can range from hardcore live-coding, to experimental demos, to inspirational talks from well-known developers. On the following days, attendees can expect 5-6 hours of intense hands-on workshops where they will build, develop and learn with experts. A party will be held on the first night of the event and breakfast and gourmet lunches will be served each day.
